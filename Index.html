Importance of Education

What is Education?

Education is the process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes.

Why Education Matters

  • Empowers individuals with knowledge.
  • Improves employment opportunities.
  • Promotes critical thinking and creativity.
  • Encourages civic participation.

Types of Education

  • Formal Education
  • Informal Education
  • Non-formal Education

Conclusion

Education is the foundation for personal and societal development.
