Your Perfect Evening Routine

Turn off screens 1 hour before bed
Dim the lights to create a calm mood
Drink a warm, caffeine-free tea
Read a book or listen to calming music
Do 5-10 mins of light stretching

Your Energizing Morning Routine

Wake up at a consistent time (even weekends!)
Avoid hitting the snooze button
Get 10-15 mins of morning sunlight
Drink a full glass of water
Eat a protein-rich breakfast within 90 mins
